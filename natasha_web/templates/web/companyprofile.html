Company Profile

Natasha Textiles is an upcoming and one of the most promising groups in Pakistan.

We are one of the leading exporters of finished fabric as well as of grey fabric in USA, Germany, U.K., Australia & New Zealand. Especially in USA and Europe we have a firm grip on the home textile products to some of the leading chain stores. IN ADDITION TO THIS WE ARE CONSIDERED AS A SPECIALIST, MOST REPUTABLE, AND RELIABLE EXPORTERS OF COMMERCIAL AND HOSPITALITY PRODUCTS TO AUSTRALIA AND USA FOR THE LAST 25 YEARS. IN AUSTRALIA WE ARE THE LEADING SUPPLIERS TO NEW SOUTH WALES GOVERNMENT.

 

Natasha Textiles is a composite unit with 80,000 spindles, 200 airjet looms and a full and complete state of the art finishing unit with a total production of about 3 million meters monthly. Natasha Textiles has also got a high-tech stitching unit which fully supports its made-ups orders. The annual export of the company is flourishing day by day and this year our total export has touched the figure of US$ 80 million. We also possess a well equipped design studio where the diffusion of lines, colour, curves and points takes a meaningful creative direction which when combine with the expertise of marketing personals, a unique creation comes into existence.

 

Above all this we strongly believe that the main strength of any organization is not the machinery they have, but it’s the management and the professionals who runs the organization. Here, we are proud to announce that its our professionals who are the main contributor in taking our organization to such heights. Our philosophy is that its not only the quality which leads in you long term business relation but it’s the commitment and dedication towards achieving this goal. Flexibility towards accommodating our customers has always been a key factor in our strong relationship with the customer.

The management is very keen in exploring more opportunities and expands its span of business. The enthusiastic management office has given further boost to the company and the company’s evolvement graph has shown a steep upward curve.
